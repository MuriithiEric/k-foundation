Who We are

Kreatives Foundation is a leading innovative African youth-based creative arts and technology organization dedicated to empowering the creative youth of Africa with a special focus on vulnerable youth through the fusion of creativity and technology. Our mission is not just words; it's a call to action, a dedication to transform young lives and communities for the better.

How We Work

Our Easy Steps (1-4)

  • Creative Expression
  • Creative Culture
  • Creative Spaces
  • Driving Innovation
Encourage and support the creation of innovative content by providing resources, mentorship, and platforms for young creatives to express themselves through digital arts and technology.
Foster an environment where creativity is celebrated and valued. Develop programs and initiatives that inspire individuals to explore and develop their creative potential.

Create physical and virtual spaces where young creatives can collaborate, experiment, and showcase their work. Establish Kreatives-Hub centers as vibrant hubs for creative exploration.

Integrate cutting-edge technology into educational programs and initiatives, ensuring that youth are exposed to and proficient in the latest digital tools.

Our Easy Steps(5-8)

  • Problem-Solving
  • Entrepreneurship
  • Holistic Development
  • Thriving Community

Develop initiatives that challenge young minds to solve real-world problems through creative thinking. Emphasize the importance of innovation as a key skill for personal and societal advancement.

Support and incubate entrepreneurial ventures in the digital arts sector. Encourage young creatives to turn their innovative ideas into sustainable businesses.

Go beyond skills training and contribute to the holistic development of youth. Ensure that programs address mental well-being, leadership skills, and personal growth, enabling them to thrive in all aspects of life.

Build a supportive community where creatives, mentors, partners, and supporters come together to share knowledge, resources, and experiences. Create a network that amplifies the collective impact of the foundation.

Measure success not only by individual achievements but also by the positive impact on communities and the environment. Implement initiatives that contribute to the Sustainable Development Goals and create a lasting influence.

Mission & Vision

Mission

"Our mission at Kreatives Foundation is to empower African youth by leveraging digital creative arts and technology to raise awareness, inspire innovation, and drive positive change in their communities and beyond. Through our programs, we equip young individuals with the skills, resources, and opportunities they need to become leaders and catalysts for social and economic transformation."

Vision

By reducing youth unemployment and education deprivation through the power of digital arts and technology, Kreatives Foundation envisions: a future where African youth not only create and innovate but also thrive as empowered, creative leaders contributing to positive change in their communities and beyond. Through our mission and goals, we strive to be a catalyst for the holistic development of the next generation.
