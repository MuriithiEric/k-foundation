Learn About Our Programs

BigPicture

The BigPicture Initiative aims to empower underprivileged youth through experiential learning. By establishing "BigPicture Clubs," the program provides young people with hands-on experience in photography, digital arts, and technology.

GoodLight

Good Light Focuses on Behavioral rehabilitation through creative digital arts leverages the therapeutic and educational benefits of digital technologies. Digital art platforms offer creative outlets for emotional expression, helping individuals process trauma and promote mental health.

Get-Kreative

Get Kreative Initiative focuses on workshops and training sessions bolster entrepreneurship in Africa's informal sector and gig economy by teaching financial literacy, digital marketing, and business management. Using practical, context-specific knowledge and hands-on activities, they foster innovation and problem-solving.

Key Components

  • Hands-On Learning
  • Mentorship
  • Professional Bridge
  • Scholarships
Practical and hands-on learning experiences in digital arts, photography, and videography. This hands-on approach enables individuals to acquire real-world skills that are highly valuable in the professional sphere.
Mentorship opportunities to guide participants in skill development and career navigation. Additionally, involvement in project-based impact activities empowers individuals to apply their knowledge in meaningful ways.
Connecting education with professional opportunities, the initiative offers gap year students options, career guidance, internships, mentorships, and career placements. This ensures a seamless transition for participants from the learning phase to practical application.
The program plans to offer scholarships to deserving individuals. Project-based impact competitions provide a platform for participants to showcase their skills, contributing to positive change. Additionally, there will be a photo gallery exhibition showcasing the creative works of participants.
